Welcome to the showcase of my research!

At present, I am experimenting on Pool Boiling. Objective of this research is to enhance critical heat flux(CHF) in pool boiling applications. Enhancement of CHF can be performed in a number of ways.

  • Surface Modification
  • Nano-fluid addition

Surface modifications are done by providing nano-particle coating of different thickness, and externally installed structures.
